Introduction to ReadsYs

ReadsYs is an application designed to manage reinsurance business in a very simple and practical way. Reinsurance is a type of insurance that an insurer buys in onther to transfer some or all of its own risk on a policy or a group of policies. Through reinsurance for example, a reinsurer reimburses a life insurer for the death benefits the insurer pays to a policyowner's beneficiaries.

An insurance company or an insurer uses reinsurance to transfer part or all of a risk to another insurance company or reinsurer. In exchange for the reinsurance, the insurer pays reinsurance premiums to other reinsurer. The amount of the reinsurance premiums is based on the reinsurance arrangement negotiated between the two companies.

As long as the reinsurance arrangement is in effect, the sellers of the reinsurance reimburses the buyer for claims covered unthe the arrangement. Every insured risk must be supported by reserves, which are accounts that an insurer maintains to set aside fund for meeting future business obligation, including paying claims. An insurer is responsible for setting up sufficient reserves to cover its risk.When risks are reinsured, the reinsurer also agrees to establish reserves for part or all of the reinsured portion of each risk.

Those above are subject that all about ReadsYs will manage through out this system. The access is control on 3 dropdown buttons. The basic consept is to received a facultatively offer or a cessie that ceded under automatic treaty, all the process are electronically manage. Data are integratedly organized to preserve a consistent presence through out the whole mecanism thar are available on 3 main menu. The menu is represented by 3 dropdown button. Select a category and then click it to activate process. To be able to access a task, user should has a valid authority. Validity of authority is control by giving an interval time period to every user that has sign in.

Actuarial

As the primary responsibility of the actuary and hence the acturial department is to protect the interest of the policyholders. ReadsYs look at what this mean in practical term and the function that the acturial department should carry out.

The primary interest of the policyholders is that the office should be able to pay the claims when it become due. This mean the office must be solvent. So in practical terms the responsibilities of the actuary boil down to ensuring that the office is currently solvent and can be expected to remain so in the future. The first step in this process is to examine the current position by carrying out an investigation into the liabilities and the assets of the office. Office should be valued periodically by actuary.

ReadsYs is designed to help actuary to work out all that task describe above in an easy and simple way. Menu Actuarial consist of tasks :Performance, Valuation, Reserves, Security Loading, Retention Limit.

Performance

The are four main areas where the shareholders, managers, and directors of an office require information on its progress. The information should be interrelated, and it is important that all the measuring system are consistent and base on consistent data. This is often a problem for offices as they develope system for different puposes that work independently of each other and sometimes give conflicting answer. It is not a good idea to have an information system that tell the manager that the office is on course for along-term profitable future when the regulator or supervisory authority tells the managers that they have to cease writing new business. ReadsYs is designed to help actuary to eleminate thise problems.

Performance that ReadsYs manage are responsive respons for users to 4 main questions:

1. Is the office solvent ?

2. Is the office still in a position to fullfill its long term objective regarding capital requirements and profits.

3. Is the office performing well or badly compared to targets the directors and managers have set the office for the foresseable future?

4. Is the day to day performance satisfactory and what are the current trends?

ReadsYs will provide a visual clues that users can easyly understand its figurative meaning as early warning. This kind perseption is supported with details and comprehensive data and its chart.

II. Inforce of Reinsurace Adalah terminologi yang digunakan oleh ReadsYs untuk proses pemutahiran pertanggungan reasuransi atau lebih umum dikenal dengan sebutan proses renewal.

Setelah pertanggungan dinyatakan dapat di reasuransikan, underwriter atau reinsurance analyst akan memberi status kepada cessie pertanggungan dengan nilai INFORCE sedangkan status renewalnya akan dimutahirkan menjadi bulan dan tahun renewal berikutnya (contoh January2014). Jika ReadsYs dijalankan pada waktu ulang tahun pertanggungan (yakni pada keadaan dimana bulan issue date sama dengan bulan proses) dan bahwa terdapat sekelompok cessie yang memenuhi kriteria renewal maka ReadsYs akan segera melakukan pengumpulan cessie dari semua kelompok pertanggungan tersebut. Hasilnya dikelompokkan berdasarkan customer. Kemudian ReadsYs membuatkan tabel job.

Tabel job yang dibuat akan ditempatkan pada tab job development, yakni pada repositori inforced jobs, repositori documents, repositori outstanding inforce jobs dan repositori inforce job bulan sekarang.

Untuk mengontrol agar proses renewal teradministrasikan dengan baik sesuai dengan kriteria renewal, ReadsYs menyediakan satu tab khusus; Renewal Control on Data Master.

Managing Business' Document

ReadsYs integrates information that a business document has and information notes from underwriting process, because the underwriting decision making sometimes is decided after a certain period of time. This is a condition that potentially raise an intonsistency for medical advicer, underwriter, and CMO. Therefore managing business document together with placing new offer on the same page is important.

Business Documents are manage likely library mecanism, every call, mail, wording or treaty is treated as document, all have to be recorded as physical object, the objects are place in cabinet, racks, folders, and files. ReadsYs then function as a tools of mapping physical object to electronic data. ReadsYs will provide library address of physical object document. To take out and take in users have to follow a requisition procedure. Each document has level of security which mean access on electronic data and physical object is protected under a rule that company has. A very high level security is protected for only owner has authority to access both electronic and physical object.
