Operating Procedures

Introduction

FAS is colection of customer's billing ; It is consist of begining balance which is an ending balance of previous accounting period, current transaction during the accounting period. This application will be used to view and create.

There are 5 closed panels. Activate the panels.

5 Years FAS Development Indicator

This panel will show a five year summary data of FAS. Each of them display its endding balance and an indicator sign.

[1] Click the panel. [2] Select a period and click the glyphicon. [3] Find the result on a grid and an executive summary. [4] Select a customer and click it to see details on a modal.

Counting Billing into FAS

This is a list of billing that has been classfied through Managing Billing process. It is an ongoing process jobs of reinsurance transactionnd.

[1] Click the panel. [2] Select a Customer and click it to see more detail information on customer's transaction.
