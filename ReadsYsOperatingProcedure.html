ReadsYs is an application designed to manage reinsurance business in a very simple and practical way. Reinsurance is a type of insurance that an insurer buys in onther to transfer some or all of its own risk on a policy or a group of policies. Through reinsurance for example, a reinsurer reimburses a life insurer for the death benefits the insurer pays to a policyowner's beneficiaries.

An insurance company or an insurer uses reinsurance to transfer part or all of a risk to another insurance company or reinsurer. In exchange for the reinsurance, the insurer pays reinsurance premiums to other reinsurer. The amount of the reinsurance premiums is based on the reinsurance arrangement negotiated between the two companies.

As long as the reinsurance arrangement is in effect, the sellers of the reinsurance reimburses the buyer for claims covered unthe the arrangement. Every insured risk must be supported by reserves, which are accounts that an insurer maintains to set aside fund for meeting future business obligation, including paying claims. An insurer is responsible for setting up sufficient reserves to cover its risk.When risks are reinsured, the reinsurer also agrees to establish reserves for part or all of the reinsured portion of each risk.
